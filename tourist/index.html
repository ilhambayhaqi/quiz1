Tourist Attaction

Lapindo Hot Mud

On May 29, 2006, mud and steaming hot water squirted up in a rice field in Porong, Sidoarjo. The disaster has taken dozens of villages in Sidoarjo. But after that tragedy, it become a tourist spot that can be visited while in Sidoarjo.

Delta Fishing

For those whose hobby is fishing, the Delta Fishing would be the right option. It offers not only a soothing ambiance but also complete facilities. This place is highly recommended for family travelers and ideal for spending the special occasion.

Kali Porong Tour

Sidoarjo is the capital of East Java split by two rivers i.e. Kali Porong and Kali Mas; this is the reason it is called the city of Delta. Kali Porong is not only used for the reservoir, but its existence has been an important sector for tourism

Pari Temple

Within walking distance from Lapindo Hot Mud, it sits a magnificent historic temple of the Kingdom of Majapahit, built when Hayam Wuruk King reigned. The ancient structure is called Pari temple, differing from the other Majapahit remains. The architecture is the mix of Khmer and Thai designs.
